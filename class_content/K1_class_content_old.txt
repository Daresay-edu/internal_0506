Unit 1 Letters & Toys

1-2课时
Unit1 Letters课程计划
第一单元学习内容如下：
1）自我介绍
    您在家里可以跟宝贝这样沟通：
    Hello, what's your name?
    引导宝贝说 I'm Elly/Billy/Scott/Eddy/Angel/Baron. 或者My name is Elly/Billy/Scott/Eddy/Angel/Baron.
    让孩子尽快对自己的英文名字熟悉起来~
    孩子们做Presentation时说：
    Hello, everyone~ I'm ... I'm X years old. 
    I'm a girl/boy. This is my work. It's a ...
    I like it. Do you like it?
    
2）Classroom Rules 课堂规则：
    English Only. /No Chinese.
    Be quiet please. Be good listeners.
    No running. Walk slowly.
    No fighting.
    No littering. 
    Say thank you.
    有问题要 Hands up.
    想喝水要说 May I drink some water.
    想去洗手间 May I go to bathroom.    

3）字母A-Z大写小写及肢体展示；
    您在家可以引导孩子认识字母及大小写：
    What's this letter?
    引导孩子说“It's letter A/B/C/D.”
    It's an upper case A. It's a lower case a. 
     
4）数学课I know big and small. 引导孩子明白大和小。
比如 This bed is big, this bed is small. 
Mommy's shoes are big. My shoes are small.

Unit 2 Colors



19-20课时
Unit2 Colors课程计划
从今天开始我们学习一个科学实验课，我们yellow, blue和red混合出 orange, green和purple三种颜色。

1）颜色我们学习了 red, blue, yellow三原色，以及white 和black， grey.
     您可以在家里引导孩子“What's this color”/"What color is it?" 让孩子说“It's red/blue/,,,”
     或者问“What's this/What is it?” 引导孩子说“It's a grey butterfly/green watermelon...”
2) 还有科学实验，red+blue=purple          red+yellow=orange    blue+yellow=green
                             white+red=pink             black+yellow=brown
     您可以在家引导孩子“What makes purple?” 让孩子回答“Red and blue makes purple.”
     或者“What color is it when  red and blue mix together?” or "What can red and blue make?"   让孩子回答“Red and blue makes purple.”
3）日常会话： Hi, boys and girls. Hello, … Goodbye. See you later. 
4）动作指令：Come here. Turn around. 
5）School Supplies 五种常用的学习用品：paper, glue, tape, scissors, paint. 
6）学习字母A,B,C,D所代表的四个单词，引导孩子们理解 A is for apple. B is for bird. C is for cat. D is for dog.

Lesson 1 red, blue, yellow.

您可以利用家里或者平时带你宝贝儿出去玩儿的环境，引导宝贝儿辨认周围的颜色，引导宝贝儿用英文表达颜色。
