主要内容如下:
1.学习了高频词汇I我,并学会用I 造句.关键句是I have a big family.
2.用看图说话的方式,学习了关键词hop单足跳跃,read读书,play玩,paint画画,cookie曲奇饼.
3.学习了一首歌曲The wheels on the bus  
了解公交车上的wheels车轮people车上坐的人, wiper雨刷,horn车喇叭. 
以及他们的特征,圆圆的车轮,颠上颠下的人,及雨刷和车喇叭的声音.
歌词如下:
The wheels on the bus go round and round,round and round,round and round.  
The wheels on the bus go round and round.All day long. 
The people on the bus go up and down,up and down up and down. 
The people on the bus go up and down.All day long. 
The wiper on the bus goes swish swish swish,swish swish swish,swish swish swish.
The wiper on the bus goes swish swish swish.All day long.
The horn on the bus goes honk honk honk, honk honk honk,honk honk honk. 
The horn on the bus goes honk honk honk.All day long.

本节课我们学习 9-10课时 Math数学课 Chapter 1. Lesson 7-9 greater than, less than
主要学习内容如下:
1.学习’>’greater than sign大于号,引导孩子们做出手势,更形象的理解.
2.学习大于的概念5>3
(1)There are three orange rings. There are five purple rings. Click on the pole that has more rings.
(2)There are more purple rings than orange rings. We can see five is more than three.
(3)So five is two more than three. So we can use > this sign to show five is greater than three.
(4)The sign points to the number that is small—three.
3.生词有:Construction worker建筑工人,roof屋顶,tile瓷砖,brick砖,bulb灯泡,fence栅栏,nail钉子
